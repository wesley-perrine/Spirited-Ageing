About Us


Spirited Ageing LLC was founded in 2002 by Barb Wenger in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida with the express purpose to share her knowledge and expertise about building wine cellars.


Barb has seen many mistakes in Florida wine cellars made by homeowners, builders and contractors. She wants you to benefit from her experience and avoid the pitfalls, especially the hard to fix mistakes that occur behind the walls.
